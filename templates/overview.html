About the Dataset & Application

Database Context: This dashboard is powered by cleaned sales transaction records from unilever_sales.csv, representing 31,437 rows of invoice details spanning a dense 9-day transactional window from 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-09. The backend pipeline cleans numeric fields (such as Net Amount and Sales Value) by stripping currency formatting and commas before executing analytics.

App Objectives: The platform helps visualize core performance trends, analyze customer loyalty, and predict churn. Users can filter invoices dynamically to update KPI metrics, calculate custom RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) value cohorts, and utilize Random Forest and XGBoost classifiers to forecast customer churn risk in real time.
