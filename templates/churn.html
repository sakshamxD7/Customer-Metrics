Predictive Churn Definitions & Model Pipeline

Operational Churn Definition: In this transaction timeline, churn is defined by an inactivity window of 3 days or more relative to the maximum timeline date (2026-06-09). Any customer whose last purchase occurred on or before June 6th, 2026 is labeled as Churned (1); otherwise, they are classified as Active (0).

Machine Learning Setup: We fit two models on-the-fly—Random Forest and XGBoost—utilizing 26 behavioral features (e.g., total spend, order count, variety of brands/categories, and channel distributions). We completely exclude recency to prevent target leakage. The models output individual probabilities, categorizing customers into High, Medium, and Low Risk levels.
